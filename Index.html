The Brutal Truth

Why 97% of Authors Never Reach More Than 500 Readers

The brutal truth about book marketing that nobody wants to tell you — but you need to hear if you're serious about success.

Problem 01

The Visibility Death Spiral

You launch with excitement, get 15-40 sales from people who already know you, then Amazon's window closes. Your ranking plummets from #15,000 to #350,000. Sales drop to 0-3 per day. The algorithm has decided your book isn't worth showing. You're now competing with 4 million titles for scraps of attention — and losing.

Problem 02

The Random Tactics Trap

Monday: Instagram post (12 likes, 0 sales). Wednesday: Facebook ad ($50 spent, 1 sale). Friday: Promo site submission (rejected). This is "Random Acts of Marketing" — disconnected tactics with no strategic architecture, no compounding effects, no systematic visibility. It's exhausting, demoralizing, and financially devastating.

Problem 03

The Platform Dependency Trap

Building your entire business on Amazon-only distribution, Facebook-dependent traffic, or BookBub as your only source isn't a business — it's a house of cards. One policy change, one algorithm update, one rejected submission, and your income disappears overnight. You need to own your traffic through strategic infrastructure.

Problem 04

The Launch-and-Abandon Mistake

The first 30 days determine your book's entire algorithmic future. If you abandon promotional efforts after week 3, Amazon sets your "natural" baseline at 2 copies/day — permanently. That baseline becomes nearly impossible to change. Book marketing isn't an event. It's permanent infrastructure running 24/7/365.

Problem 05

The "One Book at a Time" Blindspot

Your entire catalog is a visibility ecosystem where each book amplifies the others. Readers who discover Book 3 go back and buy Books 1-2. Free Book 1 drives full-price sales of Books 2-7. Email lists built from all books create instant launch velocity. Each new release triggers Amazon to re-promote your entire catalog. But this only works with catalog-level visibility infrastructure — not individual book-level tactics.

The Gaps

How Many Readers Are You Losing Every Month?

Right now, thousands of readers are searching for books exactly like yours. They're ready to buy. They have credit cards out. And they will never find you.

Gap 01

Category Positioning Gap

You're competing in categories with 64,000+ titles where you'll never reach page 1. Strategic repositioning to winnable categories can increase organic discovery by 10.5X overnight — same book, same sales, dramatically more visibility.

Gap 02

Algorithm Momentum Gap

Amazon rewards momentum (acceleration) not absolute numbers. A book going from 5 to 15 sales/day gets more promotion than one consistently selling 30/day. Most authors create flat velocity. Infrastructure creates deliberate acceleration spikes.

Gap 03

Reader Capture Gap

For every 100 readers who discover your book, 85-95 will never buy from you again. With strategic reader magnet infrastructure capturing at 50-70%, the same sales generate $72,180 more over 3 years — purely from owned audience.

Gap 04

Network Isolation Gap

Successful authors leverage partner networks that 10-50X their reach. Newsletter swaps, cross-promotion groups, and reader magnet bundles give access to 100,000-500,000 targeted readers. Solo authors can't reach them.

Gap 05

Sustainability Gap

Promotional spikes followed by collapse = wasted momentum. Infrastructure-based authors see 5X more annual sales because each effort builds on the last instead of starting from zero. Compound growth beats sprint-and-crash every time.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with me — answered honestly.

How do you communicate with clients?

All communication is conducted via email. This ensures complete documentation of all strategies, recommendations, timelines, and results. You'll have a permanent record of everything — fully transparent and accountable. I respond within 24 hours on weekdays.

What genres do you work with?

I work with all fiction and non-fiction genres across 47+ categories. My infrastructure approach adapts to genre-specific requirements — what works for romance differs from thrillers, business books, fantasy, and more. The strategic framework stays consistent while tactical execution varies by genre.

Do you guarantee results?

I guarantee execution of agreed-upon strategy and infrastructure building. Specific sales numbers or rankings depend on variables outside my control (algorithm changes, market conditions, book quality, timing). However, properly built visibility infrastructure consistently generates significantly better results than no infrastructure or random tactics.

How long does it take to see results?

Some elements show improvement within 7-14 days (category repositioning, metadata optimization). Others build over 30-90 days (email list growth, partnership networks). Complete visibility infrastructure typically shows compound results over 3-6 months. This is systematic infrastructure that builds permanent competitive advantages, not a quick-fix promotion.

What if I'm a brand new author with no reviews?

We can still build infrastructure. I'll recommend the optimal sequence: start with elements that don't require reviews (category positioning, metadata, reader magnet setup), implement ethical review strategies, then layer in tactics requiring review thresholds. Infrastructure is MORE critical when starting from zero, not less.

What if I've tried marketing before and it didn't work?

Most authors who "tried marketing" used random disconnected tactics without systematic infrastructure. The question isn't "does marketing work" — it's "what specific infrastructure does YOUR book need?" Failed tactics reveal what NOT to do, which helps identify what WILL work. Email me what you tried — I can usually pinpoint exactly why it failed.

How is this different from other book marketing services?

Most services offer one-off promotional events ("we'll email your book to our list"). These create temporary spikes without permanent infrastructure. I build systematic visibility architecture that generates ongoing discovery long after any individual event. It's the difference between renting visibility for a week versus owning visibility infrastructure permanently.

How much does it cost?

Every author's situation is unique — different genre, positioning, goals, and needs. I build custom infrastructure specific to YOUR book. Email me your book details and goals, and I'll respond with honest assessment, strategic recommendations, and transparent investment details tailored to your specific situation. No hidden fees, no surprises.
